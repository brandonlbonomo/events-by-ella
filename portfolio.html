Our Portfolio

Stories Told Through Craft

Every event is a new story. Browse the celebrations we’ve brought to life — each one designed from scratch and built entirely by hand.

Garden Reception

We rescued this vintage bicycle from an antique market, restored it by hand, and transformed it into a living floral display — weaving fresh roses, trailing ivy, and wild greenery through every spoke and curve. It became the centerpiece guests photographed most.

Blush & Gold

Every letter in the oversized “MRS” display was hand-constructed and filled with preserved florals in the bride’s chosen blush palette. The dessert table was curated to match — gold-dusted macarons, custom cookies, and a tiered cake surrounded by hand-placed details.

Preppy Plaid

This welcome banner was hand-sewn from plaid fabric, with each letter individually cut and stitched. We paired it with a hand-painted rocking horse motif and fresh blue hydrangeas arranged in a vintage brass vessel sourced specifically for this celebration.

Custom Decor

We hand-bent wire into a dress form, then meticulously attached hundreds of individual silk and preserved flowers to create a one-of-a-kind floral gown sculpture. Built entirely in our studio, this piece took shape over days of careful, patient craftsmanship.

Styled Tablescape

Each setting was hand-placed with pink glass goblets, gold charger plates, and custom-folded napkins. The birdcage centerpieces were sourced from antique shops, cleaned and restored by hand, then filled with fresh florals and trailing greenery.

Sweet Table

We designed every element of this dessert display — from the custom-ordered teddy bear cake to the hand-decorated cookies featuring plaid patterns and baby motifs. Each tier and tray was carefully styled to create a cohesive, abundant spread that felt both elegant and whimsical.

Miss to Mrs

This complete bridal shower transformation included a hand-built floral arch backdrop, vintage dress displays styled with real lace, a custom vanity mirror station, and hand-lettered signage throughout. Every element was designed, sourced, and assembled by our team from start to finish.

Classic Elegance

The gold mirror bar menu was hand-lettered in our calligraphy style, framed with antique birdcages filled with fresh florals. We designed this reception corner to feel like a discovered moment — intimate, refined, and intentionally imperfect in the way only handmade things can be.

Tablescape Detail

A close look at the handcrafted details that make each place setting feel personal: antique birdcage centerpieces filled with garden roses, individual gold-rimmed place cards hand-written in calligraphy, and pink crystal goblets that catch the light just right.

Escort Card Display

We transformed an ornate antique birdcage into an escort card station, draping it with lush greenery and setting it atop hand-draped olive velvet. Each card was hand-written and clipped with care — a small detail that sets the tone for the entire evening.

Full Room Styling

This complete baby shower transformation shows what it looks like when every element is designed as part of one vision: navy velvet linens with hand-sewn plaid runners, a baby clothes clothesline strung across the room, brass desk lamps repurposed as table accents, and handcrafted ABC block centerpieces at every seat.

Place Setting Detail

Every guest found a hand-assembled place setting waiting for them: a wooden charger plate, a plaid menu card designed and printed in house, a hand-painted ABC block at each seat, and a personalized name card written in our calligraphy. It’s these cumulative details that make a celebration feel truly personal.

Floral Arch Backdrop

We built this arched backdrop frame from scratch in our studio, then covered it in hand-placed florals and trailing greenery. Paired with a white bench and plush rug, it became the bridal shower’s signature photo moment — a piece guests lined up to stand in front of all afternoon.

Styled Long Table

Navy velvet linens were hand-draped along the full length of this table, topped with plaid runners we sewed in our studio. Each place was anchored by hand-stacked ABC block centerpieces, fresh blue hydrangeas, and brass accents sourced from vintage shops — a preppy plaid vision brought to life from end to end.

Balloon Backdrop & Carriage

A vintage wicker carriage we found and restored became the anchor of this celebration display. We surrounded it with a hand-assembled balloon garland in soft pastels, fresh floral arrangements, and greenery — creating a show-stopping photo backdrop that doubled as the room’s centerpiece.

Backdrop Closeup

A closer look at the custom photo backdrop we designed for a preppy plaid baby shower: a hand-painted name panel framed by a balloon garland in deep navy, hunter green, and cream. Every balloon was sized and placed by hand to create a natural, organic flow around the central signage.

Book Station

We styled this guest book station using a vintage suitcase as the base, topped with a Polo teddy bear, hand-knit baby booties, and a hand-lettered sign inviting guests to leave a note. Small details like these turn a simple station into a keepsake-worthy moment.

Entry Display

The first thing guests saw when they walked in: a full entry display featuring our hand-sewn plaid welcome banner, a vintage book station, a baby clothes clothesline strung with tiny outfits, and a hand-lettered framed sign. We designed every element to set the tone before guests even reached the main room.

Place Setting Close-Up

A detail shot that shows the layers of craft at each seat: a hand-designed game card, hand-stacked ABC blocks, fresh hydrangea stems in vintage brass, and a personalized favor — all carefully arranged to make every guest feel like the celebration was made just for them.
