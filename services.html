Celebrations Crafted by Hand

From intimate showers to grand receptions, we plan, design, and build every detail — so your event tells a story only you could tell.

Weddings

Your love story, told through every handcrafted detail

Your wedding is more than a day — it’s a feeling. We design and build weddings from the ground up: custom ceremony backdrops constructed in our studio, hand-lettered mirror signage, centerpieces built with real and preserved florals, and styled tablescapes that make every guest feel the intention behind each setting. From full planning through day-of coordination, we handle the logistics so you can be fully present for every vow, every toast, every first dance.

Bridal Showers

The celebration before the celebration, made unforgettable

A bridal shower should feel like a love letter to the bride-to-be. We design themed showers with handcrafted floral arch backdrops, custom “Miss to Mrs” lettering, styled vanity displays, and curated dessert tables where even the signage is hand-painted. Every piece — from the welcome sign at the door to the favor tags at each seat — is designed and assembled in our studio, giving the entire celebration a warmth and personality that store-bought decor simply cannot match.

Theme Design Handmade Details Custom Backdrops Party Favors Venue Setup

Baby Showers

Welcoming a new chapter with handmade warmth

Every new arrival deserves a celebration as special as the moment it marks. We build baby showers from scratch: hand-sewn plaid table runners paired with navy velvet linens, ABC block centerpieces stacked and styled by hand, clothesline displays strung with tiny outfits, and dessert tables anchored by custom-ordered cakes surrounded by our hand-decorated cookie displays. From preppy plaid to woodland whimsy to modern minimalist, we design each shower around the family’s story.

Custom Themes Handmade Centerpieces Balloon Designs Sweet Tables Welcome Displays

Casual Parties

Because every reason to celebrate is a reason to do it beautifully

Birthdays, milestones, anniversaries, graduations — we believe every gathering deserves the same level of care as a formal event. We style casual parties with custom balloon garlands, handmade signage, themed dessert displays, and vintage prop styling that transforms any backyard or venue into something your guests will talk about for weeks. The word “casual” doesn’t mean cutting corners — it means creating a relaxed, joyful atmosphere with intentional, handcrafted details at every turn.

Custom Celebrations

If you can dream it, we can build it

Have something completely unique in mind? That’s where we truly come alive. We’ve built vintage floral bicycles as garden party decor, constructed wire mannequins draped in handmade floral dresses, and designed birdcage escort card stations from antique finds. We handle the concept development, source and build every element, and deliver a one-of-a-kind experience that exists nowhere else in the world — because we made it just for you.
