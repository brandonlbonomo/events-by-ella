Our Process

From Vision to Celebration

Every event we create follows a thoughtful process designed to bring your ideas to life — without the stress.

1

Discovery

We start with a conversation. Tell us your vision, your story, your must-haves — and we’ll listen to every detail.

2

Design

We craft a custom concept — colors, textures, layouts, and decor plans — all designed around you and presented for your approval.

3

Build

Our studio comes alive. Centerpieces are shaped, backdrops are constructed, signage is hand-lettered — every piece made with intention.

4

Celebrate

We handle every detail on the day — setup, styling, coordination, cleanup — so you can be fully present for every moment.

✿

Bespoke Design

No templates. No catalogs. Every event begins with your story — your colors, your memories, your vision. We translate it all into a custom design concept created exclusively for you, from the color palette down to the napkin fold.

✍

Handmade Decor

Nearly everything we bring to your event is crafted in our studio. Centerpieces, backdrops, signage, place settings — each piece is shaped, assembled, and finished by our hands. That is the difference you can see and feel.

★

Coast-to-Coast Service

Headquartered on Long Island, New York, we bring our hands-on, made-with-love approach to celebrations in all 50 states. Distance doesn’t change our commitment to craft — we arrive with the same care, no matter the zip code.
